Quarterly Financial Report Q4 2025

Executive Summary

This report presents the financial performance for the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2025. Overall revenue grew by 12% year-over-year, driven primarily by strong performance in the Digital Services and Cloud Infrastructure segments.

Revenue Breakdown

By Segment

Segment Q4 2025 ($M) Q4 2024 ($M) YoY Change
Digital Services 4,250 3,680 +15.5%
Cloud Infrastructure 3,120 2,810 +11.0%
Consulting 2,890 2,750 +5.1%
Legacy Systems 1,340 1,520 -11.8%

By Region

North America contributed 58% of total revenue, followed by EMEA at 27% and APAC at 15%. The APAC region showed the strongest growth at 18% year-over-year.

Key Metrics

Profitability

  • Gross Margin: 42.3% (up from 40.1%)
  • Operating Margin: 18.7% (up from 17.2%)
  • Net Income: $1.85B (up from $1.62B)

Cash Flow

Operating cash flow for the quarter was $2.1B, representing a 14% increase from the prior year period. Free cash flow conversion remained strong at 92%.

Strategic Initiatives

AI and Automation

The company invested $450M in AI research and development during Q4, bringing the full-year R&D spend to $1.6B. Key milestones include:

  1. Launch of the GenAI Platform v2.0 with enterprise-grade security
  2. Deployment of automated document processing across 12 client engagements
  3. Partnership with three leading universities for AI talent pipeline

Sustainability

Carbon emissions were reduced by 8% quarter-over-quarter. The company is on track to meet its 2030 net-zero commitment. Data centre energy efficiency improved by 12% through adoption of liquid cooling technology.

Risk Factors

Key risks identified during the quarter include:

  • Macroeconomic uncertainty affecting client discretionary spending
  • Increased competition in the cloud infrastructure market
  • Regulatory changes in data privacy across multiple jurisdictions
  • Foreign exchange headwinds, particularly EUR/USD volatility

Outlook

For Q1 2026, the company expects revenue in the range of $12.0B to $12.4B, representing 10-13% year-over-year growth. Operating margin is expected to remain stable at approximately 18-19%.

Management reaffirms the full-year 2026 guidance of $49B to $51B in revenue, with continued investment in AI capabilities and geographic expansion.
